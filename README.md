Visualising the History of Nobel Prize Winners

Python/pandas analysis of Nobel Prize data. Finds the most common gender and birth country of winners, identifies decades with the highest US-born and female winner ratios, determines the first female laureate, tracks female winners by decade, and lists people who won multiple Nobel Prizes.

Python project using pandas to analyze Nobel Prize data and uncover trends in laureates across gender, country, decade, and category.

Features
Finds the most common gender and birth country of winners
Identifies the decade with the highest proportion of US-born laureates
Calculates which decade/category had the highest share of female winners
Finds the first female Nobel Prize winner in the dataset
Lists individuals who won multiple Nobel Prizes
